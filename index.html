Explore Lanzhou, a bustling metropolis and a pivotal node city along the ancient Silk Road. Explore the vibrant night markets, marvel at the intricate designs of the White Pagoda, and savor the unique flavors of Lanzhou's renowned hand-pulled noodles. Jiayuguan City, a captivating node along the historic Silk Road, beckons travelers to uncover its rich tapestry of history and culture.
